GigPad

GigPad is a live performance companion built for working musicians.

Whether you're performing in pubs, weddings, clubs or festivals, GigPad keeps your songs, setlists and gigs organised so you can focus on your performance.

Features

  • Create and organise your own song library
  • Build reusable setlists
  • Schedule upcoming gigs
  • Live performance mode
  • Auto-scrolling lyrics
  • ChordPro support
  • Lyrics only mode
  • Chords over lyrics mode
  • Song requests during a performance
  • Offline database
  • Fast and lightweight

Perfect for:

  • Solo performers
  • Acoustic musicians
  • Covers bands
  • Wedding bands
  • Open mic performers
  • No subscriptions.
  • No cloud account required.
  • Your music stays on your device.

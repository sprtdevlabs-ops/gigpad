How to Convert Chords Over Lyrics to ChordPro

Our free text conversion tool automatically scans your documents to isolate rows containing musical keys like Cmaj7, D#m, or G/B. By computing the character-offset coordinates of each chord token relative to the sentence underneath, it embeds the musical shorthand directly into native ChordPro brackets [ ] seamlessly.

Why use the ChordPro Format?

Standard chord charts rely heavily on spaces to align notation above text. While this layout functions on full-size paper printouts, it fails on mobile devices. When lines auto-wrap on tiny screens, the chords lose alignment completely. Embellishing songs in the ChordPro framework anchors chords inside words, ensuring formatting scales dynamically across smartphones, tablet devices, and gigging performance apps.
